Sour Cream Bread

Source: Kathie Wagner

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (118ml) warm water -- water should be warm, not hot, to the touch
  • 2 packages dry yeast
  • 1 tsp. sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups (360 ml) sour cream
  • 1/2 cup (1/4 pound) butter
  • 1/2 cup (104 g) sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 5 cups (625 g) all-purpose flour
  • 3 eggs

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

Dissolve yeast and 1 tsp. sugar into warm water, and set aside to proof -- in a few minutes, the yeast should create a floating layer of foam.

In a small saucepan, heat cream and butter until the cream bubbles around the edges.

Add sugar and salt, and stir until the butter melts.

Let sour cream mixture cool until only slightly warm.

In large bowl, slightly beat the eggs, and blend in the yeast mixture.

Once sour cream mixture has cooled, add to egg mixture and blend thoroughly.

With a spatula, mix in the flour, one cup at a time.

Cover bowl loosely with plastic wrap, and let rise until doubled in volume.

Stir down and pour into 2 medium loaf pans, and let rise (again) until doubled.

Bake 30 - 35 minutes.
