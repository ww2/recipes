Port Cake

Based on: Wine Cake recipe at thepancakeprincess.com

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 tbs unsalted butter, melted
  • 2.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 3 tbs cornstarch
  • 1 tbs + 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 4 eggs
  • 3/4 cup port

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

Combine melted butter with olive oil, let cool to room temperature.

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cornstarch, baking powder, salt and nutmeg.

In a smaller bowl, whisk together eggs and oil mixture, then add the port.

Stir wet mixture into dry mixture until combined - there will probably be some smallish lumps.

Pour into a greased and floured bundt pan and bake for 45-55 minutes, or until inserted tester comes out clean.
