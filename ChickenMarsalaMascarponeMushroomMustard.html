Chicken with Marsala + Mascarpone + Mushroom + Mustard Sauce

Based on: Chicken with Mustard Mascarpone Marsala Sauce recipe at Foodnetwork.com

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into large bite-sized pieces
  • 0.5 to 0.75 tsp kosher salt
  • 0.5 to 0.75 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 lb cremini mushrooms, sliced

  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 cup dry Marsala wine
  • 8 oz mascarpone cheese
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp dried tarragon

Directions

Sprinkle the chicken with salt and pepper to taste.

Heat the oil in a heavy large dutch oven over medium heat.

Add the chicken and cook just until browned, 4 to 8.

Transfer the chicken to a plate and allow to cool.

While the chicken cools, melt 2 tablespoons of butter in the same dutch oven over medium-high heat.

Saute onion until tender, about 2 minutes.

Add the mushrooms and garlic, and saute until the mushrooms are tender and the juices evaporate, 12 - 15 minutes.

Add the wine and simmer until it is reduced by half, about 4 minutes.

Stir in the mascarpone and mustard, stirring until the mascarpone all melts.

Return the chicken and any accumulated juices to the skillet.

Stir in the tarragon.

Simmer, uncovered, over medium-low heat until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce thickens, about 2 - 8 minutes.

Notes

Pairs well with fettucine.
