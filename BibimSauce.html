Mocha Mochi

Source: Wes Price (synthesized from several other bibim sauce recipes)

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp water
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp brown rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp minced garlic

Directions

Combine thoroughly in a medium container (mason jar works fine, for this volume).
