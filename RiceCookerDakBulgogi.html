Rice Cooker Dak Bulgogi

Source: Wes Price

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp light brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin (or rice wine)
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 3 tbsp gochugang paste
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 boneless chicken thighs, cut into 1" x 0.5" x 0.5" pieces
  • -----
  • 1 1/2 cups calrose rice
  • 1 cup low sodium chicken broth
  • 4.5 oz water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • black pepper
  • 1 cup carrot, cut into thin sticks
  • 2 cup mushrooms, sliced (shiitake or cremini)

Directions

Combine everything from brown sugar down to black pepper for marinade.

Marinate chicken for 0.5 - 4 hours.

Rinse rice thoroughly.

Add water, broth, and salt to pot; stir well.

Remove chicken from marinade, draining off and discarding excess.

Place the chicken, then carrots, then mushrooms onto rice.

Turn on the rice cooker and allow it to cook until finished.
