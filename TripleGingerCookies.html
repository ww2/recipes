Port Cake

Based on: Triple Ginger Cookie recipe at Unegaminedanslacuisine.com

Ingredients

  • 1.5 C all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1.25 tsp cinnamon
  • 0.25 tsp nutmeg
  • 0.25 tsp allspice
  • 0.125 tsp cloves
  • 0.125 tsp white pepper
  • 0.25 tsp salt
  • 120 g (0.75 C) crystallized ginger, finely chopped
  • 0.5 C (4 oz) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 0.5 C dark brown sugar
  • 0.5 C unsulfured molasses
  • 3 tsp freshly grated ginger
  • 0.5 C turbinado sugar, for rolling

Directions

In a medium mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, ground ginger, cinnamon, allspice, salt and crystallized ginger.

In a large bowl, beat the butter and brown sugar together until creamy.

Add molasses and fresh ginger, beat until well combined.

Add the flour/spice mixture and beat until just combined (don't overmix).

Chill for at overnight.

Line cookie sheets with parchment paper.

Place the rolling sugar into a large flat bowl or plate.

Divide the dough into golfball-sized balls, rolling each ball in sugar and then setting aside to refrigerate again while the oven preheats.

Preheat the oven to 325F.

Place the chilled balls of dough onto the sheet, allowing for at least 2 inches of spreading room.

Bake 12 - 15 minutes or until cracks begin to develop.

Remove the sheet from the oven and allow cookies to rest for 5 minutes before carefully transferring to a cooling rack.
